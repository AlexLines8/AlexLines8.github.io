The Vienna Gambit

Every Vienna Gambit must start with:

1.e4 e5 2.Nc3 Nf6 3.f4

There are multiple variations of the Vienna Gambit. Here they are:

Accepted Decline Nc6 Decline d6 Counter d5

Vienna Gambit Accepted

1.e4 e5 2.Nc3 Nf6 3.f4 exf4

Here you play:

4.e5 Ng8 5.Nf3 d6 6.d4 dxe5 7.Qe2 Bb4 8.Qxe5 Qe7 9.Bxf4

If they insted protect the pawn with g5, develop as you would normally but then play h4.


Decline with Nc6

1.e4 e5 2.Nc3 Nf6 3.f4 Nc6

Here you play:

4. fxe5 Nxe5 5.d4 Ng6 5.e5 Ng8

From here just develop as you would with the accepted gambit. The computer also likes h4 h5 to kick away knight and then develop D.S bishop.


Decline with d6

1.e4 e5 2.Nc3 Nf6 3.f4 d6

Here you play:

4.Nf3 Nc6 5.L.S Bishop out - Pin Knight if possible. Take if attacked Bd7 6.d3 g6 7.O-O

Long term goals are to:

  • Pressure e5
  • Open up F file

Counter with d5

1.e4 e5 2.Nc3 Nf6 3.f4 d5

Here you play:

4.fxe5 Nxe4 5.Qf3
